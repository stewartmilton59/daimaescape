About Us

Welcome to Daima Escape - Your Timeless Getaway

Daima Escape offers unique and private vacation homes designed for comfort, freedom, and relaxation. Set in a calm natural environment, it's the perfect place to unwind and escape the pace of everyday life.

Nestled in a serene and well-preserved forest at the foothills of Mount Meru, Daima Escape is located just 4 kilometers from the charming town of Usa River and 3 kilometers from the entrance to Arusha National Park. Surrounded by rivers and lush greenery, it provides a tranquil retreat in Arusha—the renowned gateway to African safaris.

Our Story

A Timeless Escape in Nature

Daima Escape was born from a vision to create a sanctuary where guests could experience the true beauty of Tanzania while enjoying the comforts of a private, fully-furnished home. The name "Daima" means "timeless" or "forever" in Swahili—reflecting our commitment to providing an experience that feels both timeless and unforgettable.

Our property features a cozy one-bedroom villa for couples and a spacious two-bedroom family home accommodating up to six guests. Whether you're seeking a romantic getaway, a family adventure, or a peaceful retreat, Daima Escape offers the perfect setting.

Surrounded by rivers and lush greenery, we take pride in preserving the natural beauty that makes our location so special. We invite you to slow down, breathe deeply, and connect with nature during your stay.
