About Daima Escape

Your Timeless Getaway in Nature

Daima Escape offers unique and private vacation homes designed for comfort, freedom, and relaxation. Set in a calm natural environment at the foothills of Mount Meru, it's the perfect place to unwind and escape the pace of everyday life. Located just 4 km from Usa River town and 3 km from Arusha National Park along Momella Road.
